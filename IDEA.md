# Product Vision: "Plaintiff Intelligence Live Agent"

This is good. Now you're thinking like a **product builder**, not just a hackathon participant.

You're essentially designing:

> 🧠 A Live AI Case Intelligence Agent for Plaintiff Personal Injury (PI) firms

Let's zoom out and design the **bird's-eye product vision**.

---

## 🎯 Core Idea

A real-time AI agent that:

1. Talks with plaintiffs (live intake)
2. Organizes and summarizes evidence
3. Builds financial damage models
4. Produces settlement-ready intelligence packets

All powered by multimodal Gemini + Google Cloud.

---

## 🧭 Big Picture Flow

Think of it as 4 intelligent layers:

```
Layer 1 – Live Intake Agent
Layer 2 – Evidence Intelligence Engine
Layer 3 – Damages & Valuation Modeling
Layer 4 – Litigation-Ready Output
```

Now let's walk through each layer.

---

## 🗣️ LAYER 1: Live Intake Agent (Front-End Experience)

**User:** Plaintiff
**Mode:** Real-time voice + document upload

### What Happens

* Agent asks adaptive follow-up questions
* Plaintiff uploads:
  * Medical records
  * Accident photos
  * Insurance emails
  * Police reports
* Agent builds structured timeline

### Output

* Clean incident narrative
* Timeline visualization
* Evidence indexed by date

This satisfies:
✔ Live interaction
✔ Interruptible
✔ Multimodal

---

## 📂 LAYER 2: Discovery & Evidence Intelligence Engine

This is where your "auto-review & summarize" idea lives.

### What It Does

Automatically:

* Summarizes medical records
* Highlights diagnosis & treatment gaps
* Extracts cost totals
* Flags contradictory statements
* Summarizes deposition transcripts
* Detects admissions of liability
* Summarizes insurance correspondence

Instead of 100-page PDFs →
Lawyer sees:

> "3 treatment gaps. Total medical bills: $48,200. Defendant admitted fault in email dated April 4."

🔥 That's HUGE value.

---

## 💰 LAYER 3: Financial Modeling & Damages Calculator

Now we enter strategic territory.

### Inputs

* Medical expenses
* Lost wages
* Duration of recovery
* Severity indicators
* Comparable verdict data

### Outputs

* Economic damages calculation
* Pain & suffering estimate
* Settlement range projection
* Risk-adjusted expected value

Example:

> Estimated case value range: $85,000–$130,000
> Probability of trial success: 72%

This is the "lawyer money question."

---

## 📦 LAYER 4: Litigation-Ready Evidence Packet

Final output:

A downloadable structured packet containing:

* Incident summary
* Timeline
* Evidence index
* Medical summary
* Damages calculation
* Weakness analysis
* Settlement recommendation

Basically:

> A paralegal + analyst + junior associate compressed into one report.
