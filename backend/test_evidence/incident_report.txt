INCIDENT/ACCIDENT REPORT

Date of Incident: January 15, 2025
Time: 10:45 AM
Location: 456 Industrial Avenue, Building Site C

Employee Name: Maria Santos

DESCRIPTION OF INCIDENT:
Employee Maria Santos was working on scaffolding at approximately 15 feet height 
when the scaffolding support bracket gave way. The employee fell to the ground 
level, landing on concrete surface.

INJURIES SUSTAINED:
- Fractured right arm (compound fracture)
- Bruising and contusions on right side
- Minor head laceration

WITNESSES:
1. John Martinez - Foreman (present at scene)
2. Robert Chen - Safety Coordinator (arrived within 2 minutes)

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS TAKEN:
- 911 called at 10:47 AM
- First aid administered
- Area secured and cordoned off
- Employee transported to Riverside General Hospital

EQUIPMENT INVOLVED:
- Baker scaffolding unit, Model BS-150
- Support bracket Serial #: SC-2019-4472

PRELIMINARY CAUSE:
Scaffolding support bracket failure. Equipment inspection records indicate 
bracket was last inspected 3 months prior with no issues noted.
