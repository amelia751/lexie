\section*{INCIDENT DESCRIPTION}

On February 8, 2024, at approximately 10:15 AM, employee Maria Elena Santos was performing drywall installation work on scaffolding located on the second floor of Building C, East Wing.

\textbf{Sequence of Events:}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Employee was standing on scaffolding platform approximately 15 feet above ground level
\item Employee was reaching to secure a drywall panel to the upper portion of the wall
\item The scaffolding plank beneath the employee shifted unexpectedly
\item Employee lost balance and fell from the scaffolding
\item Employee landed on concrete floor approximately 15 feet below
\item Coworkers Carlos Ramirez and James O'Brien witnessed the fall and immediately called for help
\item Site Foreman Dave Peterson called 911 at 10:17 AM
\item Paramedics arrived at 10:28 AM and transported employee to Riverside General Hospital
\end{enumerate}

\section*{INJURIES SUSTAINED}

\begin{itemize}
\item Suspected fracture to right wrist/forearm
\item Head injury (employee was conscious but disoriented)
\item Back pain
\item Multiple visible bruises and abrasions to left side of body
\end{itemize}
